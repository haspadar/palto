This Sony DVD Home Theater system features a five-disc DVD changer and receiver with a complete set of six surround speakers for high quality detailed sound. It also features an input for XM's satellite radio programming. (Separate XM subscription required.) One can also play sounds from an iPod-type device through a separate iPod dock or wireless PC music player. (Not included)

What is included are a pair of 32” left and right speakers, a single floor “woofer” speaker, two “tweeter” speakers that can be wall mounted, and a center “bar” speaker. The main unit (receiver) also has a built-in digital audio tuner that can be preset for up to 30 AM and FM radio stations that can be set and controlled through its wireless remote.

This setup was recently extracted from an estate that previously had this system arranged as part of a home theater. If one presently has a large TV monitor, adding this system will enhance one’s viewing for an experience worth its capture!
