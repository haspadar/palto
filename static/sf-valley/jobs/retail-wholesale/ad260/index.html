Home Consignment Center in Calabasas is hiring!
This is a great opportunity for that person that is looking for a unique sales position! We are a high-end furniture consignment business and need new consignment specialists to join our fun and friendly team!
You will view and price pieces customers bring in for consignment. After COVID-19 waiting periods, you will also be going out to the beautiful homes in the area to choose and price furniture and decor to then sell in our showroom!
Are you passionate about interior design and furniture, maybe even an HGTV junkie?
Can you strike up a conversation with anyone you meet?
Fast-paced environment, no holding up the counters in this job! Meet new people and curate beautiful pieces! The variety is endless!
We reward our employees' hard work with monthly bonuses based on the store's sales numbers. Team work is HIGHLY encouraged.
Must have necessary computer skills to learn our simple point-of-sale (POS) system.
Some weekend days required.
Our hours are awesome. Shifts are 9:45am-6:15pm Monday through Saturday, Sunday shifts are 11:45am to 5:15pm! Sleep in AND be home in the evenings!

Submit your resume, or stop by the store! If you do not have relevant experience on your resume, please submit a note explaining why you would be great for this job.
