Job Summary

The Lawrence J. Ellison Institute is seeking a talented and passionate Physician Assistant or Nurse Practitioner to join its team. As part of the Ellison Institute, the PA/NP is responsible for performing diagnostic, therapeutic, preventive and health maintenance services under the direction and supervision of a physician. The PA/NP will provide guidance and assistance to the healthcare team in maintaining optimum quality of care of wellness patients. The Institute is made up of a diverse group of innovative and talented professionals who work in a fast-paced environment that provides ongoing support to research projects, patient care, lab research and administrative operations.



The PA/NP is responsible for performing the following job duties:
