Summary

The Floor Manager is responsible for the overall customer experience and must ensure high standards of service, compliance, cleanliness, and professionalism, and must convey positive company optics at all times. They will manage inventory control to ensure seamless service, and lead the budtending and reception team, supporting them through ideas, best practices and attainment of results.


Description

Uphold inventory control to minimize loss by complying with all BCC and company inventory control procedures, product waste procedures and vendor delivery procedures.
Maintain all company standards including store selling floor cleanliness, back room organization, visual presentation and safety standards at all times
Assist in daily store recovery, restocking, and store sanitation.
Oversee all team members on the floor, and enforce company standards, guidelines, and regulations.
Handle customer returns, issues and complaints in a proactive and professional manner with customer retention always in mind.
Actively pursue brand and product knowledge through company and vendor provided tools and resources.
Ensure all team members are compliant with all BCC regulations
Possess strong knowledge of BCC regulations and manage product handling and compliance at the point of sale.
Possess strong knowledge of Metrc and manage inventory counts and restocking, as well as upholding inventory control to minimize loss by complying with all BCC and company inventory control procedures, product waste procedures, defectives and returns procedures, and vendor delivery procedures.
Promote and abide by all company policies, procedures, and loss prevention practices.

Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
Knowledge of Metrc and BCC compliance.

Leadership skills, and ability to train, course-correct, and coach team members.
Work well under pressure and deadlines; ability to multitask.
