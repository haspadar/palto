Asbestos and Lead Analysts (PCM, PLM, or TEM)

AmeriSci Los Angeles has immediate positions available for Asbestos and Lead Analysts. Several Shifts Available.

For over 33 years, AmeriSci has been providing testing services for various markets throughout the world. We provide technical expertise and knowledge related to Environmental Testing. Our mission is to partner with our customers in building their business. We do this by providing outstanding service and the highest quality data available. We have locations in Manhattan NY, Boston MA, Los Angeles CA, and Richmond VA.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

Preparation and analysis of air and bulk samples for asbestos
Visually determine the percentage and optical properties of asbestos and non-asbestos material
Complete QA/QC practices per company QA/QC manual
Calibration and maintenance of laboratory equipment
General laboratory maintenance
Other laboratory duties may be assigned by management as needed

Requirements:

HS degree/equivalent or Associates/Bachelor's Degree
1-2 years of previous academic or commercial lab experience a plus
Must have excellent communication, computer, typing, and organizational skills.
Candidate must be able to distinguish colors in order to perform analysis.
Must be able to work independently and with a team
Must be able to multi-task, and prioritize assignments

Benefits:

Company paid medical insurance
Company paid dental insurance
Company paid life and AD & D insurance
401K retirement plan
Company paid vacation
Company paid sick days
Company paid holidays
Optional short-term disability insurance
Optional long-term disability insurance
