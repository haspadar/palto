Offering a desk space (about 500 sq feet) in a larger studio (about 1200 sq feet). The studio is on 6th and Mill in the Arts District near Pizzanista, Blue Bottle, Guerilla Tacos, the Artist Supply Store etc. It is nice and clean, gets great west facing sunlight and has a really nice view of downtown LA. You would be getting the corner of a big room shared with two other people, and my studio is in the back behind a wall. The shared room is big enough to shoot in if you're a photographer (9 ft seamless with whole set up etc, table top, whatever....). I have built a divider wall which is on wheels to section off your zone if you'd like more privacy or a place to hang prints etc.

There are two photographers (one of which is me) and a collage artist (who also does photography). 4 people total.

With covid, we generally wear masks whenever we are there with another person, and there is a big window at the front of the studio that stays open when one of us is in to let plenty of air in. It is very rare that there are more than two people there at once, and very often you will have the space to yourself. I think as of now we have all had both doses so no mask may be necessary in the studio but the building requires you to wear one in common spaces.

We are looking for someone who is very chill who will use the space about as much as the rest of us...which sometimes is 2-3 days a week, sometimes 5 days a week depending on how busy we are. Sometimes we have shoots there and if we can we'll get the client to pay for the space so we can put that money towards the rent. We take turns putting beers in the fridge. I'll bring snacks occasionally. We could maybe use some more plants...

Ideally looking for another photographer, illustrator, designer, editor, or someone who doesn't work in a medium that creates alot of dust/mess. If you are a sculptor who makes things out of string or think you may be an exception to the above statement then you should inquire too! Just nothing that creates dust.

There is a small fridge, a shared closet, a couch, a record player/stereo and a nice bluetooth speaker.

Bathroom is shared and is down the hall (has a slop sink)

There is street parking and if you are in a pinch there is a paid lot attached to the building.

There is a passenger elevator and a freight elevator and two sets of stairs. I usually take the stairs, but the elevators are handy for moving stuff in and out of the studio etc.

We also have a mailbox in the mailroom, there is a key that stays in the studio so anyone can get a package there.

Building is 24 hours.

No Heat or AC but there are only a few days in the summer where it gets unbearable.

Rent has gone up 50$ a year since I've been there so I would assume in Sept it will do the same, but between 4 people it's not so bad...like 12 bucks.

6 month minimum lease agreement. First and security to move in so $1,000.00 total. Security returned when 30 day notification of moving out is given.
