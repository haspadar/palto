Retail Store Team Member - Sticker Planet Store at Farmers Market in Los Angeles, central LA

We are seeking a part-time team member for approximately 15-20 hours per week for both weekend and afternoon/evening weekday shifts. Our family-owned store is located in the historic Original Farmers Market at 3rd and Fairfax in Los Angeles (adjacent to The Grove) and is a popular destination for both locals and tourists from all over the world.

Our store is a unique and fun shopping environment, selling artistic stickers for kids and adults. Our customers include parents, grandparents, children, teachers and anyone who enjoys our creative products.

Candidates should enjoy helping customers, communicate well and demonstrate a high level of maturity and responsibility. Punctuality and excellent job attendance are critical. The job responsibilities include helping customers find products that meet their needs, educating them about our products and ringing up sales, preparing customer orders, keeping the store clean and organized, and receiving and restocking inventory. Our employees also must follow and enforce all Farmers Market and Sticker Planet COVID protection procedures which we take seriously.

We are looking for people who desire a long-term employment relationship. Our employees typically stay with us for years and take a great deal of pride in the store's appearance and operation. Unlike some corporate or big-box retail jobs, our employees wear many hats and have an opportunity to learn different aspects of running a store while gaining valuable experience in the retail field.

Starting salary is $14.50 per hour during training with a raise for completion of training with good performance. Once hired, we give employees regular shifts with reliable hours that rarely change so you can plan activities, such as school, around your work schedule. All employees receive paid sick days.

If our store sounds like a good fit, we'd love to hear from you! Be sure to include a resume and a personalized cover letter that tells a bit about you, why you feel you are a good candidate for this job and why you are interested in working for our store. In your cover letter, please confirm that you are seeking a year-round position. We are not looking to hire seasonal workers. Please reply to this listing only via email (jobs.stickerplanetLA@gmail.com).

We will contact you if we feel you may be a good fit for the position. Please do not phone the store directly. We encourage you to visit our store's homepage at www.stickerplanet.com.

We look forward to hearing from you!
